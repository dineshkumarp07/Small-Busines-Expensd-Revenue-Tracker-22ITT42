About Expense and Revenue Tracker

Welcome to the Expense and Revenue Tracker application! This tool is designed to help you manage your finances effectively by tracking your expenses and revenues in one place.

With this application, you can:

  • Add and categorize your expenses.
  • Record your revenues and categorize them.
  • View all your financial data in a single dashboard.
  • Edit or delete entries as needed.

Our goal is to provide a simple and intuitive interface to help you stay on top of your finances. Whether you're managing personal expenses or tracking business revenues, this application is here to make your life easier.

Start using the Expense and Revenue Tracker today and take control of your financial future!
